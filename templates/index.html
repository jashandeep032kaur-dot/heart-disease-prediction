Dataset Columns & Importance

male

1 = Male, 0 = Female. Important because men have higher risk for heart disease.

age

Age in years. Very important predictor as risk increases with age.

education

Education level. Moderate importance; reflects lifestyle and awareness.

currentSmoker

1 = Currently smoking, 0 = Not. Smoking increases heart disease risk but partially captured by cigsPerDay.

cigsPerDay

Number of cigarettes per day. Used in model to quantify smoking intensity.

BPMeds

1 = On blood pressure meds, 0 = No. Used in model as high BP is risk factor.

prevalentStroke

1 = History of stroke, 0 = No. Low occurrence; excluded from model for simplicity.

prevalentHyp

1 = Hypertension history, 0 = No. Related to BP meds; excluded to avoid redundancy.

diabetes

1 = Diabetes, 0 = No. Used in model as a major risk factor.

totChol

Total cholesterol in mg/dL. Used in model for lipid profile info.

sysBP

Systolic blood pressure. Used in model for hypertension risk.

BMI

Body Mass Index. Used in model for obesity risk factor.

glucose

Blood glucose level. Used in model to detect diabetes-related risk.
