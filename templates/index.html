üìä Dataset Columns & Importance

Sex_male

1 = Male, 0 = Female. Important: Men have higher risk of heart disease.

age

Age in years. Very important: Risk increases with age.

cigsPerDay

Number of cigarettes smoked daily. Used: Higher smoking increases risk.

BPMeds

BPMeds indicates whether a person is currently taking medication to control high blood pressure.

1 = On blood pressure meds, 0 = No. Used: High BP is a risk factor.

diabetes

1 = Yes, 0 = No. Used: Diabetes significantly raises risk.

BMI

Body Mass Index. Used: Obesity is a risk factor for heart disease.

totChol

Total cholesterol in mg/dL. Used: High cholesterol increases risk.

sysBP

Systolic blood pressure is the pressure in your arteries when your heart beats (contracts) and pumps blood into the arteries.

Systolic blood pressure. Used: High BP is a key risk factor.

glucose

Blood glucose level. Used: Elevated glucose indicates diabetes risk.

prevalentStroke

1 = History of stroke, 0 = No. Not used in model: Rare and partially redundant.

prevalentHyp

1 = Hypertension history, 0 = No. Not used: Already captured by BPMeds & sysBP.

currentSmoker

1 = Currently smoking, 0 = No. Not used: Partially captured by cigsPerDay.

education

Education level. Dropped: Minimal effect on prediction; excluded to simplify model.
