Dataset Columns & Importance

Sex_male

1 = Male, 0 = Female. Important because men have higher risk.

age

Age in years. Very important as risk increases with age.

cigsPerDay

Number of cigarettes smoked daily. Used in model to quantify smoking intensity.

BPMeds

1 = On blood pressure meds, 0 = No. Used as high BP is a risk factor.

diabetes

1 = Yes, 0 = No. Used as a major risk factor for heart disease.

BMI

Body Mass Index. Used for obesity risk factor.

totChol

Total cholesterol in mg/dL. Used to assess lipid-related risk.

sysBP

Systolic blood pressure. Used for hypertension-related risk.

glucose

Blood glucose level. Used to detect diabetes-related risk.

prevalentStroke

1 = History of stroke, 0 = No. Low occurrence; excluded from model.

prevalentHyp

1 = Hypertension history, 0 = No. Excluded to avoid redundancy with BPMeds and sysBP.

currentSmoker

1 = Currently smoking, 0 = No. Partially captured by cigsPerDay; excluded from model.

education

Education level. Moderate importance; excluded to simplify model.
